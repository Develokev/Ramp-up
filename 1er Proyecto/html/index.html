Concrete Surfing is an "aprecciation post website" born in Madrid in 2023, with the intention to post info about the Surfskate movement, specifically in Spain. The goal is to gather pro and amateur content so we can share it with the rest of the skating community.

We present to you information regarding national talent, upcoming events, surfcamps, boards, city spots, and much more. We encourage you to send your material, as we'll be more than happy to show it!
